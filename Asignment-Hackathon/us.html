Who are we?

We are the ones who were once lost, like you. Django is slowly changing the world. Through thoughts on topics that range from polination and general bee-life (from the perspective of an intruder larva), to tea-time in 'ancient alien' Rome, he has created a larger set of word documents than any person as far as we know of so far.
The stories, the guides, the ponderings, the deep analysis of his own farts (pungent and fear inducing), now ask us again who we are!
We are his children.

What you should know about Django.

He is loving and sensitive, but fair and stern like Bear Grills.
After a high-flying career as a door-to-door salesman he finally heard a primordial call from a place far beyond himself. It beckoned him with an intensity that he has - to this day - only felt while standing in front of a Berlin kebab-shop.
He started traveling the world from east to west, north to south, South-America, Asia, Africa and Europe where he finally crossed the Dutch border from Switzerland.
There he meditated under a mountain the dutch called the 'Boterberg' (though nowadays nobody speaks of it anymore, in the early nineties it was a formidable size).

After three weeks of walking on sandels and pondering the universe and the public transport system he came to the startling conclusion that the universe was in him and therefore...

He had all the answers!

You're less then 10 clicks away from finding out what these answers are.
